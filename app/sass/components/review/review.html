“Maxkravmaga is an essential training tool - extremely thorough, clear and precise”

“To truly excel in Krav Maga ( or any other martial art or form of self defense ) you must constantly train. The techniques and tactics of the system must become second nature to you. MaxKravMaga is an essential training tool for those looking to continuously improve their skills and attain a top level grasp of Krav Maga. In putting together MaxKravMaga, Eyal has created an extremely thorough, clear and precise online video training system. Here in the UK, all of my instructors are members of MaxKravMaga. This ensures they stay up to date with the techniques which they then go and teach their students. For students here in the UK, MaxKravMaga has become a widely popular training tool which they use to prepare for level grading as well as to sharpen up on techniques on a day to day basis.”

Jon Bullock / KMG UK Director
