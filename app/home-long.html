The importance of continuous training and visualization - why you must train when you are away from the gym as well…

The name of the game is practice. As you know, practice makes perfect. especially when it comes to Krav Maga training. Krav Maga is based on natural body movements and reflexes. That is why it is simple to learn and extremely effective. The more you train, practice, visualize techniques and movements.. the faster these movements will get engrained in your “muscle memory” and become nothing more than a reflex … you won’t even have to think about it before doing it.. you will just react.

Having access to my training videos gives you exactly what you need to consistently visualize and refresh techniques in your head whether at home, on the train, in the park, in various dojos, or your friends basement.. wherever you are, I’ll be there passing my knowledge directly to you.

“You can not call yourself a serious Krav Maga student or martial artist who strives to improve and advance as much as possible … without visualizing, repeating, memorizing, training, in your time off away from the gym. Supplementing your gym training with visualization training has always been a a part of the spirit of Krav Maga since Imi’s days at the beginning of when the system was formed.”

BONUS #4

Using firearms as non lethal weapons

Analysis of a variety of situations where your weapon can not be fired.

Tactical thinking of approaching situations where a firearm can not be fired: danger points, strengths, weaknesses

Weapon retention: learn how to defend, counter attack and neutralize any attempt at grabbing your weapon away from you

Understand the advantages and disadvantages that come with using youre firearm as a striking weapon

Learn lethal striking combinations incorportating your firearm

Learn to defend and neutralize complex situations when threatned with a firearm while not being able to shoot yours

Tactical thinking of dealing with a gun threat while carrying a concealed weapon

Knowing how to use a firearm as a non-lethal weapon is imperative for anyone who wants to have a well rounded self defense arsenal.

The truth is, sometimes you simply can not shoot your gun due to a number of different reasons. Some of those are….

  • Your gun jams
  • It is illegal to shoot
  • Too many bystanders
  • More..

For all of the above, it is absolutely imperative for anyone who is serious about having a complete understanding of self defense to know how to use their firearm as a blunt object / non lethal weapon.
